Submitting your video statement

Before you can proceed

The DVSA requires all candidates complete a 5 second video security statement

Why

The Driving Theory Test is an essential part of road safety. The video helps reduce instances of fraud and criminal activity.

How

We will ask you to look at the camera on this device and record you as you do so. We do not record any sound. If you are unhappy with the recording you have an opportunity to repeat it until you are satisfied.

Security
