READY: Stand in the right position

  • You need to stand away from the background to avoid shadows behind you.
  • Your head, shoulders and upper body must fit into the video.

STEADY: Keep your face clear

  • Make sure your whole face is visible.
  • Remove your glasses if you wear any.
  • Keep your hair away from your face and brushed down.
  • Headwear worn for religious or medical reasons can be left on.
